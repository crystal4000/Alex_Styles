About Me

Design is my way of
making sense of things

Hi, I'm Alex — a graphic designer based in New York with a background in fine arts and visual communication. For the past 8 years I've been helping brands find their visual voice, from scrappy startups to established names across publishing, tech, and fashion.

I care deeply about the relationship between form and meaning. Every font choice, every margin, every color has a job to do — and I love figuring out exactly what that job is.
